User Acquisition Strategies
1. Referral Program
Why it works in Zimbabwe:

Word-of-mouth is powerful in Zim's tight-knit communities

Social proof and trust transfer

Implementation:

Dual-sided rewards: Both referrer and referee get something valuable

Reward options:

Free featured listing for landlords

3 months premium agent profile for agents

Property valuation report for sellers

"Priority support" badge on listings

Easy sharing: WhatsApp share links (critical for Zim market)

Referral dashboard: Track invites, conversions, rewards earned

Limit: Cap at 10 referrals/month to prevent abuse

Example: "Refer a landlord → They list their property → You get a free 30-day featured listing"
