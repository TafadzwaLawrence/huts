1. "The Ultimate Guide to Buying Property in Zimbabwe"
Why it resonates:

Property purchase process in Zim has unique complexities (title deeds, council approvals, Cession processes)

First-time buyers often struggle with understanding transfer costs, capital gains tax, and legal requirements

Different rules for residential vs. commercial, urban vs. rural

What to include:

Step-by-step buying process (offer, agreement of sale, transfer)

Breakdown of costs: Transfer fees, conveyancing fees, stamp duty, CGT

Red flags: verifying title deeds, unpaid rates, boundary disputes

Checklist for Harare vs. Bulawayo vs. other cities (different councils, different processes)

Gate: Name + Email + Location (Harare/Bulawayo/Other)
